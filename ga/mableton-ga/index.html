Types of Home Locks

Choosing the right home lock is essential for safeguarding your property. At Lomo Locksmith, we provide expert locksmith services across all cities in Mableton Georgia offering a wide range of locks to meet your security needs. Here’s a quick guide to the most common types of home locks.
Deadbolt Locks: Deadbolts are the gold standard for home security, providing a strong barrier against forced entry. Available in single and double-cylinder variants, deadbolts are a must-have for exterior doors. They offer superior protection thanks to their solid, heavy-duty construction.
Smart Locks: For those seeking convenience and high-tech security, smart locks are a perfect choice. With keyless entry, remote access, and integration with smart home systems, these locks offer advanced security features. You can control access to your home from your smartphone, making it easy to monitor and manage who enters your property.
Knob Locks: Typically used in conjunction with deadbolts, knob locks offer basic security. They are common on interior doors but should not be relied on solely for exterior doors due to their vulnerability to forced entry.
Lever Handle Locks: Ideal for interior doors, lever handle locks are user-friendly, especially for those with mobility issues. While they are easy to operate, they are best used in low-security areas of the home.
Cam Locks: Commonly found on cabinets, mailboxes, and other less secure areas, cam locks are great for securing small spaces. They offer an extra layer of security where needed.
At Lomo Locksmith, we help you select and install the best locks for your home, ensuring peace of mind and enhanced security. Contact us today to explore the best locking solutions for your needs in Mableton Georgia .

Should I Rekey or Replace My Locks?

When faced with a security concern, many homeowners in Mableton Georgia wonder whether they should rekey or replace their locks. At Lomo Locksmith, we understand that making the right choice is essential for your peace of mind and safety.
Rekeying Your Locks
Rekeying involves altering the internal mechanism of your existing locks so that old keys no longer work. This is a cost-effective solution, especially if you’ve lost your keys, moved into a new home, or experienced a security breach. Rekeying is ideal when your locks are still in good condition but you need to ensure that only you and trusted individuals have access to your home. It's a quick and affordable way to enhance security without replacing the entire lock system.
Replacing Your Locks
Replacing your locks, on the other hand, is recommended when your locks are outdated, damaged, or compromised. This option provides an opportunity to upgrade to more secure and modern locks, such as deadbolts or smart locks. Replacing locks is essential if you've experienced a break-in or if you’re looking to improve the overall security of your property in Mableton Georgia . Mableton, GA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Mableton (/ˈmeɪbəltən/) is a city in Cobb County, Georgia, United States. Voters of the unincorporated area of Mableton approved a referendum to incorporate on November 8, 2022, and six council members were elected on March 21, 2023, with Michael Owens elected as Mayor in a runoff on April 21, 2023. According to the 2020 census, Mableton has a population of 78,000. Upon Brookhaven's cityhood in December 2012, Mableton was previously the largest unincorporated CDP in Metro Atlanta. With boundaries described in Appendix A of House Bill 839, Mableton is set to become the largest city in Cobb County in terms of population, and will include historical Mableton, along with the Six Flags area, areas of unincorporated Smyrna, and other residents of unincorporated South Cobb. While 53% of voters approved the referendum, many residents are already exploring deannexation.
