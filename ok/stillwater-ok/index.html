Types of Home Locks

When it comes to securing your home, choosing the right lock is crucial. At Lomo Locksmith, we provide expert guidance on the best types of home locks to keep your property safe and secure in Stillwater Oklahoma . Understanding the different types of locks available can help you make informed decisions for your home security needs.
Deadbolt Locks
Deadbolt locks are one of the most popular and secure options for residential properties. Known for their robust construction, deadbolts come in single, double, and keyless variants, offering excellent protection against forced entry. They are ideal for exterior doors, providing an extra layer of security beyond standard doorknob locks.
Smart Locks
For those seeking convenience and advanced security, smart locks are an excellent choice. These locks use keyless entry methods like keypads, smartphones, or biometric scans, allowing you to control access remotely. Perfect for modern homes in Stillwater Oklahoma smart locks add a tech-savvy layer of protection and are easily integrated into smart home systems.
Knob Locks
Knob locks are commonly used for interior doors and are easy to install. While not as secure as deadbolts, they are suitable for bedrooms, bathrooms, and other internal spaces. Pairing knob locks with deadbolts on exterior doors can enhance overall security.
Lever Handle Locks
Lever handle locks are often seen on interior doors and are popular for their ease of use, especially for people with mobility issues. They come in various styles, making them a functional and stylish option for your home.
At Lomo Locksmith, we help you choose the right locks to secure your home in Stillwater Oklahoma . Contact us today for expert advice and reliable locksmith services!

Should I Rekey or Replace My Locks?

When faced with a security concern, many homeowners in Stillwater Oklahoma wonder whether they should rekey or replace their locks. At Lomo Locksmith, we understand that making the right choice is essential for your peace of mind and safety.
Rekeying Your Locks
Rekeying involves altering the internal mechanism of your existing locks so that old keys no longer work. This is a cost-effective solution, especially if you’ve lost your keys, moved into a new home, or experienced a security breach. Rekeying is ideal when your locks are still in good condition but you need to ensure that only you and trusted individuals have access to your home. It's a quick and affordable way to enhance security without replacing the entire lock system.

Replacing your locks, on the other hand, is recommended when your locks are outdated, damaged, or compromised. Stillwater, OK Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Stillwater is the tenth-largest city in the U.S. state of Oklahoma. It is the county seat of Payne County, Oklahoma. It is located in north-central Oklahoma at the intersection of U.S. Route 177 and State Highway 51. As of the 2020 census, the city population was 48,394. The Stillwater Micropolitan Statistical Area had a population of 78,399 according to the 2012 census estimate. Stillwater was part of the first Oklahoma Land Run held on April 22, 1889, when the Unassigned Lands were opened for settlement and became the core of the new Oklahoma Territory. The city charter was adopted on August 24, 1889, and operates under a council-manager government system.
