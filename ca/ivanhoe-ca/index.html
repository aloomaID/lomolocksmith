Types of Home Locks

Choosing the right home lock is essential for safeguarding your property. At Lomo Locksmith, we provide expert locksmith services across all cities in Ivanhoe California offering a wide range of locks to meet your security needs. Here’s a quick guide to the most common types of home locks.
Deadbolt Locks: Deadbolts are the gold standard for home security, providing a strong barrier against forced entry. Available in single and double-cylinder variants, deadbolts are a must-have for exterior doors. They offer superior protection thanks to their solid, heavy-duty construction.
Smart Locks: For those seeking convenience and high-tech security, smart locks are a perfect choice. With keyless entry, remote access, and integration with smart home systems, these locks offer advanced security features. You can control access to your home from your smartphone, making it easy to monitor and manage who enters your property.
Knob Locks: Typically used in conjunction with deadbolts, knob locks offer basic security. They are common on interior doors but should not be relied on solely for exterior doors due to their vulnerability to forced entry.
Lever Handle Locks: Ideal for interior doors, lever handle locks are user-friendly, especially for those with mobility issues. While they are easy to operate, they are best used in low-security areas of the home.
Cam Locks: Commonly found on cabinets, mailboxes, and other less secure areas, cam locks are great for securing small spaces. They offer an extra layer of security where needed.
At Lomo Locksmith, we help you select and install the best locks for your home, ensuring peace of mind and enhanced security. Contact us today to explore the best locking solutions for your needs in Ivanhoe California .

Should I Rekey or Replace My Locks?

Deciding whether to rekey or replace your locks is an important consideration for homeowners in Ivanhoe California . At Lomo Locksmith, we understand the need for optimal home security and are here to help you make the best choice for your situation.
Rekeying Your Locks
Rekeying is a cost-effective solution that involves altering the internal pins of your existing locks so old keys no longer work. This is ideal when you’ve recently moved into a new home, lost your keys, or need to restrict access after someone moves out. Rekeying provides a fresh start without the expense of new locks, maintaining the existing hardware while enhancing security.
Replacing Your Locks
Replacing locks is recommended when your locks are damaged, outdated, or no longer meet your security needs. Opting for new locks allows you to upgrade to more secure options, such as high-security deadbolts or smart locks. This is also a great choice if you’re looking to improve the aesthetic of your doors or add advanced features like keyless entry.
Which Option is Right for You?
The decision to rekey or replace depends on your specific security needs, budget, and the condition of your existing locks. Rekeying is perfect for quick, affordable changes, while replacing offers the benefits of modern technology and enhanced protection.
