Types of Home Locks

Choosing the right home lock is essential for safeguarding your property. At Lomo Locksmith, we provide expert locksmith services across all cities in Schuylerville New York offering a wide range of locks to meet your security needs. Here’s a quick guide to the most common types of home locks.
Deadbolt Locks: Deadbolts are the gold standard for home security, providing a strong barrier against forced entry. Available in single and double-cylinder variants, deadbolts are a must-have for exterior doors. They offer superior protection thanks to their solid, heavy-duty construction.
Smart Locks: For those seeking convenience and high-tech security, smart locks are a perfect choice. With keyless entry, remote access, and integration with smart home systems, these locks offer advanced security features. You can control access to your home from your smartphone, making it easy to monitor and manage who enters your property.
Knob Locks: Typically used in conjunction with deadbolts, knob locks offer basic security. They are common on interior doors but should not be relied on solely for exterior doors due to their vulnerability to forced entry.
Lever Handle Locks: Ideal for interior doors, lever handle locks are user-friendly, especially for those with mobility issues. While they are easy to operate, they are best used in low-security areas of the home.
Cam Locks: Commonly found on cabinets, mailboxes, and other less secure areas, cam locks are great for securing small spaces. They offer an extra layer of security where needed.

Rekeying Your Locks
Rekeying involves altering the internal mechanism of your existing locks so that old keys no longer work. This is a cost-effective solution, especially if you’ve lost your keys, moved into a new home, or experienced a security breach. Rekeying is ideal when your locks are still in good condition but you need to ensure that only you and trusted individuals have access to your home. Schuylerville, NY Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Schuylerville (/ˈskaɪlərˌvɪl/) is a village in Saratoga County, New York, United States. The village is located in the northeastern part of the Town of Saratoga, east of Saratoga Springs. The Village of Victory is adjacent to Schuylerville to the southwest and the Hudson River forms the village's eastern border. The population was 1,386 at the 2010 census. The village was incorporated in 1831 and is named after the Schuyler family, a prominent family of Dutch descent in colonial America.
