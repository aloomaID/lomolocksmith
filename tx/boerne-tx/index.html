Types of Home Locks

When it comes to securing your home, choosing the right lock is crucial. At Lomo Locksmith, we provide expert guidance on the best types of home locks to keep your property safe and secure in Boerne Texas . Understanding the different types of locks available can help you make informed decisions for your home security needs.
Deadbolt Locks
Deadbolt locks are one of the most popular and secure options for residential properties. Known for their robust construction, deadbolts come in single, double, and keyless variants, offering excellent protection against forced entry. They are ideal for exterior doors, providing an extra layer of security beyond standard doorknob locks.
Smart Locks
For those seeking convenience and advanced security, smart locks are an excellent choice. These locks use keyless entry methods like keypads, smartphones, or biometric scans, allowing you to control access remotely. Perfect for modern homes in Boerne Texas smart locks add a tech-savvy layer of protection and are easily integrated into smart home systems.
Knob Locks
Knob locks are commonly used for interior doors and are easy to install. While not as secure as deadbolts, they are suitable for bedrooms, bathrooms, and other internal spaces. Pairing knob locks with deadbolts on exterior doors can enhance overall security.
Lever Handle Locks
Lever handle locks are often seen on interior doors and are popular for their ease of use, especially for people with mobility issues. They come in various styles, making them a functional and stylish option for your home.

Rekeying Your Locks
Rekeying involves altering the internal mechanism of your existing locks so that old keys no longer work. This is a cost-effective solution, especially if you’ve lost your keys, moved into a new home, or experienced a security breach. Rekeying is ideal when your locks are still in good condition but you need to ensure that only you and trusted individuals have access to your home. It's a quick and affordable way to enhance security without replacing the entire lock system.

Boerne, TX Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Boerne (/ˈbɜːrni/ BURN-ee) is a city in and the county seat of Kendall County, Texas, in the Texas Hill Country. Boerne is known for its German-Texan history, named in honor of German author and satirist Ludwig Börne by the German Founders of the town. The population of Boerne was 10,471 at the 2010 census, and in 2019 the estimated population was 18,232. The city is noted for the landmark U.S. Supreme Court case City of Boerne v. Flores. Founded in 1849 as 'Tusculum', the name was changed to 'Boerne' when the town was platted in 1852.
