Types of Home Locks

When it comes to securing your home, choosing the right lock is crucial. At Lomo Locksmith, we provide expert guidance on the best types of home locks to keep your property safe and secure in Dewitt Michigan . Understanding the different types of locks available can help you make informed decisions for your home security needs.
Deadbolt Locks
Deadbolt locks are one of the most popular and secure options for residential properties. Known for their robust construction, deadbolts come in single, double, and keyless variants, offering excellent protection against forced entry. They are ideal for exterior doors, providing an extra layer of security beyond standard doorknob locks.
Smart Locks
For those seeking convenience and advanced security, smart locks are an excellent choice. These locks use keyless entry methods like keypads, smartphones, or biometric scans, allowing you to control access remotely. Perfect for modern homes in Dewitt Michigan smart locks add a tech-savvy layer of protection and are easily integrated into smart home systems.
Knob Locks
Knob locks are commonly used for interior doors and are easy to install. While not as secure as deadbolts, they are suitable for bedrooms, bathrooms, and other internal spaces. Pairing knob locks with deadbolts on exterior doors can enhance overall security.
Lever Handle Locks
Lever handle locks are often seen on interior doors and are popular for their ease of use, especially for people with mobility issues. They come in various styles, making them a functional and stylish option for your home.

Should I Rekey or Replace My Locks?

Deciding between rekeying or replacing your locks can be a tough call for homeowners in Dewitt Michigan . At Lomo Locksmith, we understand the importance of making the right choice for your security needs. Both rekeying and replacing locks offer distinct benefits, but knowing which option is best for your situation is key to keeping your home safe.
Rekeying Your Locks
Rekeying involves changing the internal pins of your existing locks, making old keys useless while allowing you to use a new key. This option is ideal if you’ve recently moved into a new home, lost your keys, or want to prevent unauthorized access without altering your door hardware. Rekeying is cost-effective and quick, offering an immediate solution to security concerns.
Replacing Your Locks
Replacing your locks involves installing new hardware altogether, which is the best choice if your current locks are old, damaged, or you want to upgrade to a more secure option like deadbolts or smart locks. It’s also recommended if your locks have been tampered with or compromised. Replacing locks provides an opportunity to enhance your home’s overall security with modern features.
Which Option Is Right for You?
If your locks are in good condition and you’re simply concerned about key control, rekeying is a great, affordable choice. However, if you’re looking to upgrade or if the locks are damaged, replacing them is the way to go.

Dewitt, MI Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

DeWitt was named after DeWitt Clinton, Governor of New York during the 1820s. It was first settled by Captain David Scott, who moved there from Ann Arbor in 1833, and platted the land. The State Legislature formally created DeWitt Township on March 23, 1836. The first township meeting was held at the house of Captain Scott on April 8, 1836. A gentleman by the name of Welcome J. Partelo was named the township's first Supervisor at that meeting.
